Who we are

We craft synergistic partnerships with our businesses, accelerating their growth

We proactively curate and incubate high-potential businesses. We nurture them with patient funding, our years of managerial and operational expertise and access to our vast network of global relationships.

In addition, we boldly seek out promising white spaces in the marketplace and innovate to create new markets and new demand where previously none existed. In the past, we have done this both by building new ventures and strategically pivoting ones already in our portfolio.

Our key investments lie in the areas of Consumer, Life Sciences and the advancement of Sustainable Technologies.

Connect with us

At Birthstone Capital, we are committed to supporting ideas that help shape a better tomorrow.

It has been our constant endeavour to truly walk our talk. We invest our resources into patiently fostering businesses that prioritize purpose before profits. Our investments help entrepreneurs take strategic risks, especially those imperative in the early stages of business.

At its heart though, our way of doing business is centered on forming real connections and adding joie de vivre to the lives we touch along the way.

Please connect with us if you are:

  • 1

    An entrepreneur, passionate about a purpose, with a compelling business proposition (whether pre-commercial, mid-stage or distressed)

  • 3

    An investment partner who resonates with our ethos and would like to talk about potential syndication

  • 2

    A professional interested in working for one of our companies

  • 4

    An entity or an individual with an in-depth understanding of international business ecosystems and how best to navigate them

StemRx Bio Science Solution Private Limited

Industry: Life Sciences

Year of investment: 2017

www.stemrx.in

StemRx has been a forerunner in the space of regenerative medicine in India, with a strong foundation of treatments that have received both patents and regulatory approvals. The company has research projects ongoing in cutting-edge technologies such as Natural Killer (NK) Cell therapy, Tissue Nano Transfection (TNT) DNA chip and Exosome therapy. StemRx is in the process of collaborating with both American and Japanese companies for stem cell research and tech-transfer. In India, the company has state-of-the-art therapeutic centers under advanced stages of development and its expansion into international markets is imminent.
